Tunday Kebab Recipe

Procedure

  1. Prepare the Meat Mixture
    1. In a bowl, combine minced meat with spices, ginger-garlic paste, and chopped herbs.
    2. Mix well until all ingredients are thoroughly combined.
  2. Shape the Kebabs
    1. Take a small portion of the mixture and shape it into a kebab.
    2. Repeat until all the mixture is used.
  3. Cook the Kebabs
    1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
    2. Fry the kebabs until they are golden brown on both sides.
  4. Serve
    1. Transfer the kebabs to a serving plate.
    2. Garnish with onion rings and lemon wedges.
    3. Serve hot with mint chutney.

Enjoy your delicious Tunday Kebabs!

Nutrition Per Serving:

Nutrient Amount
Calories 250 kcal
Carbohydrates 10g
Proteins 20g
Fats 15g
Fibers 2g

Main Ingredients:

  • 500g Minced Meat (beef or lamb)
  • 1 large Onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 2-3 Green Chilies (finely chopped)
  • 1/2 cup Fresh Coriander Leaves (chopped)
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying
  • Optional: Lemon wedges and onion rings for garnish
