Lapsi Recipe - Madhya Pradesh

Procedure

  1. Prepare Broken Wheat
    1. Dry roast broken wheat (dalia) in a heavy pan until golden and aromatic.
    2. Set aside and let it cool completely.
  2. Make Sugar Syrup
    1. Heat ghee in the same pan and add sugar.
    2. Add water and let it boil to make a sticky syrup.
    3. Add cardamom powder and cook until one-string consistency.
  3. Combine Ingredients
    1. Add roasted broken wheat to the sugar syrup.
    2. Mix well and cook on low heat for 5-7 minutes.
    3. Add roasted nuts and mix thoroughly.
  4. Shape and Serve
    1. While still warm, shape into small balls or ladoos.
    2. Let them cool and set properly.
    3. Store in airtight container and serve as needed.

Enjoy your traditional Madhya Pradesh Lapsi!

Main Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Broken Wheat (Dalia)
  • 3/4 cup Sugar or Jaggery
  • 1/4 cup Ghee (Clarified Butter)
  • 1 tsp Cardamom Powder
  • 2 tbsp Chopped Almonds
  • 2 tbsp Chopped Cashews
  • 1 tbsp Raisins
  • 1/4 cup Water
  • Pinch of Salt
