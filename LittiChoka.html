Litti Chokha Recipe - Bihar

Procedure

  1. Prepare Litti Dough
    1. Mix wheat flour with salt and oil to form a stiff dough.
    2. Rest the dough for 30 minutes covered with a damp cloth.
  2. Make Stuffing
    1. Dry roast sattu (gram flour) until aromatic.
    2. Mix with spices, mustard oil, ginger-garlic paste, and chopped onions.
    3. Add water gradually to make a binding mixture.
  3. Shape and Cook Litti
    1. Roll dough into small circles, add stuffing, and seal properly.
    2. Cook on a clay oven or tawa until golden brown on all sides.
  4. Prepare Chokha and Serve
    1. Roast eggplant, tomatoes, and potatoes over fire.
    2. Mash them with mustard oil, onions, and spices.
    3. Serve hot litti with chokha and ghee.

Enjoy your traditional Bihar Litti Chokha!

Main Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Wheat Flour
  • 1 cup Sattu (Roasted Gram Flour)
  • 2 Eggplants (for chokha)
  • 3 Tomatoes (for chokha)
  • 2 Potatoes (for chokha)
  • 2 tbsp Mustard Oil
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 2 Green Chilies (chopped)
  • 1 tsp Carom Seeds (Ajwain)
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 large Onion (chopped)
  • Fresh Coriander Leaves
  • Ghee for serving
  • Salt to taste
