Iromba Recipe

Procedure

  1. Boil vegetables and mash them.
  2. Add fermented fish and serve with rice.

Enjoy your Iromba!

Nutrition Per Serving:

Nutrient Amount
Calories 300 kcal
Carbohydrates 40g
Proteins 15g
Fats 10g
Fibers 5g

Main Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Mixed Vegetables
  • 100g Fermented Fish
  • Salt to taste
