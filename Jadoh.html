Jadoh Recipe

Procedure

  1. Cook rice with meat and spices.
  2. Serve hot with salad.

Enjoy your Jadoh!

Nutrition Per Serving:

Nutrient Amount
Calories 450 kcal
Carbohydrates 70g
Proteins 25g
Fats 15g
Fibers 4g

Main Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Rice
  • 200g Meat (Chicken or Pork)
  • Spices: Salt, Ginger, Garlic
