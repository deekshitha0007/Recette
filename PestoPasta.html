Pesto Pasta Recipe

Procedure

  1. Make Fresh Pesto
    1. In a food processor, pulse garlic and pine nuts until roughly chopped.
    2. Add fresh basil leaves and pulse until finely chopped.
    3. While processing, slowly add olive oil until smooth paste forms.
    4. Stir in grated Parmesan cheese and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Cook the Pasta
    1. Bring salted water to boil in a large pot.
    2. Cook pasta according to package directions until al dente.
    3. Reserve 1/2 cup pasta cooking water before draining.
  3. Combine Pesto and Pasta
    1. Return drained pasta to the pot over low heat.
    2. Add pesto and toss gently to coat all pasta.
    3. Add pasta water gradually if needed for consistency.
  4. Serve and Garnish
    1. Transfer to serving bowls immediately.
    2. Top with extra Parmesan cheese and pine nuts.
    3. Garnish with fresh basil leaves.

Enjoy your fresh and aromatic Pesto Pasta!

Nutrition Per Serving:

Nutrient Amount
Calories 480 kcal
Carbohydrates 52g
Proteins 14g
Fats 24g
Fibers 4g

Main Ingredients:

  • 400g Pasta (penne or linguine)
  • 2 cups Fresh Basil Leaves
  • 3 cloves Garlic
  • 1/3 cup Pine Nuts
  • 1/2 cup Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan Cheese (grated)
  • Salt and Black Pepper to taste
  • Optional: Cherry Tomatoes, Mozzarella balls
