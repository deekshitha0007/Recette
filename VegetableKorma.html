Vegetable Korma Recipe

Procedure

  1. Prepare the Vegetables
    1. Chop all vegetables into bite-sized pieces.
    2. Blanch harder vegetables like cauliflower and carrots until tender.
  2. Make the Korma Paste
    1. Soak cashews, coconut, and poppy seeds in warm water.
    2. Blend into a smooth paste with ginger and green chilies.
  3. Build the Base
    1. Heat ghee and add whole spices until fragrant.
    2. Add onions and saut√© until golden brown.
    3. Add the korma paste and cook until oil separates.
  4. Combine and Finish
    1. Add vegetables, yogurt, and enough water for consistency.
    2. Simmer for 10-15 minutes, garnish with cilantro and mint.

Enjoy your homemade Vegetable Korma!

Nutrient Amount

Main Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Mixed Vegetables (cauliflower, carrots, peas, beans)
  • 1/3 cup Cashews
  • 1/4 cup Fresh Coconut
  • 1 tbsp Poppy Seeds
  • 3 tbsp Ghee
  • 2 Bay Leaves
  • 4 Green Cardamom
  • 1 large Onion (sliced)
  • 1/4 cup Yogurt
  • 2 inch Ginger, 2 Green Chilies
  • Spices: Turmeric, Red Chili Powder, Garam Masala, Salt
