Banana Oatmeal Pancakes Recipe

Procedure

  1. Prepare the Oat Flour
    1. Place rolled oats in a blender or food processor.
    2. Blend until oats form a fine flour-like consistency.
    3. Set aside in a mixing bowl.
  2. Make the Wet Mixture
    1. In a bowl, mash ripe bananas until smooth.
    2. Whisk in eggs, milk, vanilla extract, and honey.
    3. Mix until well combined.
  3. Combine Ingredients
    1. Add oat flour, baking powder, cinnamon, and salt to wet ingredients.
    2. Stir gently until just combined - don't overmix.
    3. Let batter rest for 5 minutes to thicken.
  4. Cook the Pancakes
    1. Heat a non-stick pan or griddle over medium heat.
    2. Lightly grease with oil or butter.
    3. Pour 1/4 cup batter per pancake and cook for 2-3 minutes until bubbles form.
    4. Flip and cook for another 1-2 minutes until golden brown.

Enjoy your healthy and delicious Banana Oatmeal Pancakes!

Main Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Rolled Oats
  • 2 large Ripe Bananas (mashed)
  • 2 large Eggs
  • 1/2 cup Milk (dairy or plant-based)
  • 2 tbsp Honey or Maple Syrup
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 1 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Ground Cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp Salt
  • Coconut Oil or Butter for cooking
  • Optional toppings: Fresh berries, sliced banana, maple syrup, nuts
