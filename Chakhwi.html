Chakhwi Recipe

Procedure

  1. Cook rice with fish and spices.
  2. Serve with green chutney.

Enjoy your Chakhwi!

Nutrition Per Serving:

Nutrient Amount
Calories 400 kcal
Carbohydrates 60g
Proteins 20g
Fats 10g
Fibers 3g

Main Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Rice
  • 200g Fish
  • Spices: Salt, Turmeric, Green Chilies
