Classic Alfredo Pasta Recipe

Procedure

  1. Cook the Pasta
    1. Bring a large pot of salted water to boil.
    2. Cook fettuccine according to package instructions until al dente.
    3. Reserve 1 cup pasta water before draining.
  2. Prepare the Alfredo Sauce
    1. In a large pan, melt butter over medium heat.
    2. Add minced garlic and saut√© for 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Make the Cream Sauce
    1. Pour in heavy cream and bring to a gentle simmer.
    2. Gradually whisk in grated Parmesan cheese until smooth.
    3. Season with salt, pepper, and nutmeg.
  4. Combine and Serve
    1. Add cooked pasta to the sauce and toss gently.
    2. Add pasta water if needed for consistency.
    3. Garnish with fresh parsley and extra Parmesan.

Enjoy your creamy Classic Alfredo Pasta!

Main Ingredients:

  • 400g Fettuccine Pasta
  • 1/2 cup Butter
  • 1 cup Heavy Cream
  • 1 1/2 cups Parmesan Cheese (grated)
  • 4 cloves Garlic (minced)
  • 1/4 cup Fresh Parsley (chopped)
  • 1/4 tsp Nutmeg
  • Salt and Black Pepper to taste
  • Optional: Grilled Chicken or Shrimp
