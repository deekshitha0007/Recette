Lal Maas Recipe - Rajasthan

Procedure

  1. Prepare the Meat
    1. Cut mutton into medium pieces and wash thoroughly.
    2. Marinate with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, and salt for 30 minutes.
  2. Make Spice Paste
    1. Soak Mathania red chilies in warm water for 20 minutes.
    2. Grind with garlic and ginger to make a smooth paste.
  3. Cook the Curry
    1. Heat mustard oil in heavy-bottomed pot until smoking.
    2. Add marinated meat and cook on high heat until brown.
    3. Add spice paste and cook until oil separates.
    4. Add water and simmer covered for 45-60 minutes.
  4. Finish and Serve
    1. Adjust seasoning and consistency.
    2. Garnish with fresh coriander.
    3. Serve hot with bajra roti or rice.

Enjoy your fiery Rajasthani Lal Maas!

Main Ingredients:

  • 1 kg Mutton (cut into pieces)
  • 15-20 Mathania Red Chilies
  • 1 cup Yogurt
  • 2 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 4-5 Garlic Cloves
  • 1 inch Ginger
  • 3 tbsp Mustard Oil
  • 1 tsp Cumin Seeds
  • 2 Bay Leaves
  • 4-5 Green Cardamom
  • 1 Black Cardamom
  • 1 inch Cinnamon
  • Fresh Coriander Leaves
  • Salt to taste
