Masor Tenga Recipe - Assam

Procedure

  1. Prepare the Fish
    1. Clean fish pieces and marinate with turmeric and salt.
    2. Pan fry the fish lightly until golden, set aside.
  2. Prepare Tenga Base
    1. Soak tamarind in warm water and extract thick paste.
    2. Alternatively, use tomatoes or elephant apple for sourness.
    3. Prepare paste by grinding ginger, garlic, and green chilies.
  3. Cook the Curry
    1. Heat mustard oil in a pan until smoking, then cool slightly.
    2. Add panch phoron (five spice mix) and let it splutter.
    3. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 30 seconds.
    4. Add tomatoes and cook until soft and mushy.
  4. Final Assembly
    1. Add tamarind water and bring to boil.
    2. Add fried fish pieces gently to the curry.
    3. Season with salt and simmer for 5-7 minutes.
    4. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve with rice.

Enjoy the tangy Assamese fish curry Masor Tenga!

Main Ingredients:

  • 500g Fresh Fish (Rohu or Catla), cut into pieces
  • 2 large Tomatoes (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp Tamarind Paste
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 4-5 Green Chilies
  • 1 tsp Panch Phoron (Five Spice Mix)
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 3 tbsp Mustard Oil
  • 1 tsp Sugar
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh Coriander Leaves
  • Water as needed
