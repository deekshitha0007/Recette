Chainsoo Recipe

Procedure

  1. Soak black gram overnight and grind to a paste.
  2. Cook with spices and serve with rice.

Enjoy your Chainsoo!

Nutrition Per Serving:

Nutrient Amount
Calories 250 kcal
Carbohydrates 30g
Proteins 15g
Fats 5g
Fibers 6g

Main Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Black Gram
  • 1 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • Salt to taste
