Zu Recipe - Mizoram

Procedure

  1. Soak rice for 4-6 hours and steam it.
  2. Cool steamed rice and mix with rice powder starter culture.
  3. Keep in a closed container for fermentation about 3-4 days.
  4. Strain the liquid and serve chilled.

Enjoy your traditional Mizoram Zu!
