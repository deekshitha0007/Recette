Poha Recipe

Procedure

  1. Prepare the Poha
    1. Rinse poha in water and drain immediately. Let it sit for 2-3 minutes to soften.
    2. Sprinkle a pinch of salt and turmeric over the poha and mix gently.
  2. Prepare the Tempering
    1. Heat oil in a pan and add mustard seeds. Let them splutter.
    2. Add cumin seeds, curry leaves, and green chilies.
  3. Add Vegetables
    1. Add chopped onions and saut√© until translucent.
    2. Add ginger, peanuts, and potato cubes. Cook until potatoes are tender.
    3. Season with salt and turmeric powder.
  4. Combine and Serve
    1. Add the prepared poha to the pan and mix gently.
    2. Cook for 2-3 minutes on low heat.
    3. Garnish with fresh coriander, lemon juice, and serve hot.

Enjoy your delicious Poha!

Main Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Poha (Flattened Rice)
  • 1 large Onion (chopped)
  • 1 Potato (cubed)
  • 2-3 Green Chilies
  • 1 inch Ginger (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp Oil
  • 1/4 cup Peanuts
  • 10-12 Curry Leaves
  • Spices: Mustard Seeds, Cumin Seeds, Turmeric, Salt
  • Fresh Coriander & Lemon Juice for garnish
