Mango Lassi Mocktail Recipe

Procedure

  1. Prepare Mango Puree
    1. Peel and chop ripe mangoes into chunks.
    2. Blend mango chunks until smooth and creamy.
    3. Set aside 2 tablespoons for garnish.
  2. Make the Lassi Base
    1. In a blender, combine yogurt, mango puree, and honey.
    2. Add cardamom powder and a pinch of salt.
    3. Blend until smooth and frothy.
  3. Add the Fizz
    1. Add ice cubes to the blender and pulse briefly.
    2. Pour the mixture into serving glasses.
    3. Top with sparkling water for the fizz.
  4. Garnish and Serve
    1. Swirl reserved mango puree on top.
    2. Garnish with chopped pistachios and mint leaves.
    3. Serve immediately with a colorful straw.

Enjoy your creamy Mango Lassi Mocktail!

Nutrition Per Serving:

Nutrient Amount
Calories 150 kcal
Carbohydrates 28g
Proteins 6g
Fats 3g
Fibers 2g

Main Ingredients:

  • 2 large Ripe Mangoes
  • 1 cup Plain Yogurt
  • 2 tbsp Honey
  • 1/4 tsp Cardamom Powder
  • Pinch of Salt
  • 1/2 cup Sparkling Water
  • Ice Cubes
  • 2 tbsp Chopped Pistachios
  • Fresh Mint Leaves for garnish
