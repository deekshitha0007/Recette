Chickpea Masala Recipe

Procedure

  1. Prepare the Chickpeas
    1. Soak chickpeas overnight and pressure cook until tender.
    2. Reserve the cooking water for later use.
  2. Make the Base
    1. Heat oil and add cumin seeds, bay leaves.
    2. Saut√© onions until golden brown and caramelized.
  3. Build the Masala
    1. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook until fragrant.
    2. Add tomatoes, spices, and cook until oil separates.
    3. Add a splash of chickpea cooking water.
  4. Combine and Simmer
    1. Add cooked chickpeas to the masala.
    2. Simmer for 15-20 minutes, garnish with cilantro and ginger.

Enjoy your homemade Chickpea Masala!

Main Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Dried Chickpeas (soaked)
  • 3 tbsp Oil
  • 1 tsp Cumin Seeds
  • 2 Bay Leaves
  • 2 large Onions (chopped)
  • 4 Tomatoes (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 1 inch Fresh Ginger (julienned)
  • Fresh Cilantro
  • Spices: Turmeric, Red Chili Powder, Coriander Powder, Garam Masala, Salt
