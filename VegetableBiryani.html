Vegetable Biryani Recipe

Procedure

  1. Prepare Vegetables
    1. Wash and chop all vegetables into medium-sized pieces.
    2. Soak paneer in warm water if using.
  2. Cook the Rice
    1. Soak basmati rice for 30 minutes.
    2. Boil rice with whole spices until 70% cooked, then drain.
  3. Prepare Vegetable Curry
    1. Heat ghee and fry onions until golden brown.
    2. Add vegetables, yogurt, and spices. Cook until vegetables are tender.
    3. Add paneer and mix gently.
  4. Layer and Dum Cook
    1. Layer cooked rice over vegetables in heavy-bottomed pot.
    2. Top with fried onions, mint, saffron milk, and ghee.
    3. Cover and cook on dum for 30-40 minutes.

Enjoy your delicious Vegetable Biryani!

Nutrition Per Serving:

Nutrient Amount
Calories 380 kcal
Carbohydrates 58g
Proteins 12g
Fats 14g
Fibers 5g

Main Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Basmati Rice
  • 200g Mixed Vegetables (carrots, beans, peas, cauliflower)
  • 150g Paneer (cubed)
  • 2 large Onions (sliced)
  • 1/2 cup Yogurt
  • 4 tbsp Ghee
  • 1/4 cup Mint Leaves
  • 1/4 tsp Saffron
  • Spices: Bay leaves, Cinnamon, Cardamom, Cloves, Red Chili Powder, Turmeric, Garam Masala, Salt
