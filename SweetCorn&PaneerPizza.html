Sweet Corn & Paneer Pizza Recipe

Procedure

  1. Prepare Pizza Base
    1. Use store-bought or homemade pizza dough.
    2. Roll into a circle and place on greased pizza tray.
    3. Brush edges with olive oil or melted butter.
  2. Prepare Toppings
    1. Cut paneer into small cubes and lightly sauté.
    2. Boil sweet corn kernels until tender.
    3. Slice onions, bell peppers, and tomatoes.
  3. Assemble the Pizza
    1. Preheat oven to 450°F (230°C).
    2. Spread pizza sauce evenly on the base.
    3. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese generously.
    4. Add paneer cubes, corn, onions, and bell peppers.
  4. Bake and Serve
    1. Bake for 15-18 minutes until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
    2. Sprinkle oregano and chili flakes on top.
    3. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot.

Enjoy your delicious Corn & Paneer Pizza!

Nutrition Per Slice (8 slices):

Nutrient Amount
Calories 320 kcal
Carbohydrates 38g
Proteins 15g
Fats 13g
Fibers 3g

Main Ingredients:

  • 1 Pizza Base (12 inch)
  • 1/2 cup Pizza Sauce
  • 200g Paneer (cubed)
  • 1/2 cup Sweet Corn Kernels
  • 1 1/2 cups Mozzarella Cheese
  • 1 medium Onion (sliced)
  • 1/2 Bell Pepper (sliced)
  • 1 Tomato (sliced)
  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  • Oregano, Chili Flakes
  • Fresh Coriander for garnish
