Coconut Lime Spritzer Recipe

Procedure

  1. Prepare Lime Syrup
    1. Heat water and sugar in a saucepan until sugar dissolves.
    2. Add fresh lime juice and lime zest.
    3. Simmer for 2 minutes and let it cool completely.
  2. Mix the Base
    1. In a pitcher, combine coconut water and lime syrup.
    2. Add a pinch of salt to enhance the flavors.
    3. Stir well and chill in refrigerator.
  3. Prepare Glasses
    1. Rim glasses with lime juice and coconut flakes if desired.
    2. Fill glasses with ice cubes.
    3. Add fresh mint leaves to each glass.
  4. Serve the Spritzer
    1. Pour the coconut-lime mixture into glasses.
    2. Top with sparkling water for fizz.
    3. Garnish with lime wheels and mint sprigs.

Enjoy your tropical Coconut Lime Spritzer!

Main Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Coconut Water
  • 1/4 cup Fresh Lime Juice
  • 2 tbsp Sugar
  • 1/4 cup Water (for syrup)
  • 1 tsp Lime Zest
  • Pinch of Salt
  • 1 cup Sparkling Water
  • Ice Cubes
  • Fresh Mint Leaves
  • Lime Wheels for garnish
  • Coconut Flakes (optional, for rim)
