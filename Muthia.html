Muthia Recipe - Dadra and Nagar Haveli

Procedure

  1. Mix grated bottle gourd, chickpea flour, spices, and herbs.
  2. Shape into small cylindrical pieces.
  3. Steam the shaped muthia for 15-20 minutes.
  4. Temper with mustard seeds, curry leaves, and sesame seeds in oil.
  5. Serve warm as a snack or side dish.

Enjoy your traditional Dadra and Nagar Haveli Muthia!

Nutrition Per Serving:

Nutrient Amount
Calories 180 kcal
Carbohydrates 25g
Proteins 6g
Fats 7g

Main Ingredients:

  • Bottle gourd (grated) - 1 cup
  • Chickpea flour (besan) - 1 cup
  • Mustard seeds, sesame seeds
  • Curry leaves
  • Green chili, ginger, coriander
  • Oil for tempering
