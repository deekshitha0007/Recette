Rabadi Recipe

Procedure

  1. Boil milk and reduce it to half.
  2. Add sugar and cardamom powder.
  3. Simmer until it thickens and serve chilled.

Enjoy your Rabadi!

Nutrition Per Serving:

Nutrient Amount
Calories 300 kcal
Carbohydrates 40g
Proteins 8g
Fats 15g
Fibers 0g

Main Ingredients:

  • 1 liter Milk
  • 1/2 cup Sugar
  • 1/2 tsp Cardamom Powder
