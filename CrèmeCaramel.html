Crème Caramel Recipe

Procedure

  1. Make the caramel:
    1. Combine 1 cup sugar and ¼ cup water in a saucepan over medium heat.
    2. Let it cook without stirring until it turns a golden amber color.
    3. Quickly pour the caramel into the bottom of ramekins or a baking dish, swirling to coat the bottom evenly.
    4. Set aside to cool and harden.
  2. Prepare the custard:
    1. Preheat the oven to 325°F (160°C).
    2. In a saucepan, heat the milk (and cream if using) until warm but not boiling.
    3. In a bowl, whisk the eggs, ¾ cup sugar, vanilla extract, and salt.
    4. Slowly pour the warm milk into the egg mixture, whisking constantly to avoid curdling.
    5. Strain the custard through a sieve for a smooth texture.
  3. Bake:
    1. Pour the custard over the set caramel in the ramekins.
    2. Place ramekins in a large baking dish and fill with hot water halfway up the sides (water bath).
    3. Bake for 45–55 minutes or until the custard is just set with a slight jiggle in the center.
  4. Cool and serve:
    1. Remove ramekins from water and let cool to room temperature.
    2. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight.
    3. To serve, run a knife around the edge and invert onto a plate to release the caramel sauce.

Enjoy your homemade Crème Caramel!

Main Ingredients:

  • 1 cup (200g) granulated sugar (for caramel)
  • ¼ cup (60ml) water (for caramel)
  • 2 cups (480ml) whole milk
  • ½ cup (120ml) heavy cream (optional for richer flavor)
  • ¾ cup (150g) granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
