Apong Recipe - Arunachal Pradesh

Procedure

  1. Soak rice and millet overnight.
  2. Steam the soaked grains and cool them.
  3. Add a starter culture (local yeast) and ferment for 2-3 days.
  4. Strain the liquid and serve chilled or at room temperature.

Enjoy your traditional Arunachal Pradesh Apong!

Nutrition Per Serving:

Nutrient Amount
Calories 100 kcal
Carbohydrates 6g
Alcohol Content 4-6%

Main Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Millet
  • Water
  • Starter culture (yeast)
