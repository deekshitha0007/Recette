Carbonara Pasta Recipe

Procedure

  1. Cook the Pasta
    1. Bring a large pot of salted water to boil.
    2. Cook spaghetti until al dente according to package instructions.
    3. Reserve 1 cup pasta cooking water before draining.
  2. Prepare the Egg Mixture
    1. In a bowl, whisk together whole eggs, egg yolks, and grated Pecorino Romano.
    2. Season with freshly ground black pepper.
    3. Set aside at room temperature.
  3. Cook the Guanciale
    1. Cut guanciale into small cubes and cook in a large pan over medium heat.
    2. Cook until crispy and golden brown (about 5-7 minutes).
    3. Remove from heat but keep warm.
  4. Combine Everything
    1. Add hot drained pasta to the pan with guanciale.
    2. Remove from heat and quickly toss with egg mixture.
    3. Add pasta water gradually while tossing to create creamy sauce.
    4. Serve immediately with extra cheese and pepper.

Enjoy your authentic Italian Carbonara Pasta!

Nutrition Per Serving:

Nutrient Amount
Calories 580 kcal
Carbohydrates 48g
Proteins 24g
Fats 32g
Fibers 3g

Main Ingredients:

  • 400g Spaghetti
  • 150g Guanciale (or pancetta)
  • 2 whole Eggs
  • 2 Egg Yolks
  • 1 cup Pecorino Romano Cheese (grated)
  • Freshly Ground Black Pepper
  • Salt for pasta water
  • No cream or garlic needed!
