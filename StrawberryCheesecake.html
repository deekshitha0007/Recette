Strawberry Cheesecake Recipe

  1. Prepare the crust:
    1. Preheat oven to 325°F (160°C).
    2. Mix graham cracker crumbs, sugar, and melted butter in a bowl.
    3. Press mixture into the bottom of a 9-inch springform pan.
    4. Bake for 10 minutes, then let cool.
  2. Make the filling:
    1. In a large bowl, beat the cream cheese until smooth.
    2. Add sugar and vanilla extract; beat until combined.
    3. Beat in the eggs one at a time.
    4. Mix in sour cream and flour until smooth.
  3. Assemble and bake:
    1. Pour the filling over the cooled crust.
    2. Bake for 50–60 minutes, or until the center is almost set.
    3. Turn off the oven and let cheesecake cool inside for 1 hour.
  4. Chill the cheesecake:
    1. Remove from oven and let it cool to room temperature.
    2. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  5. Add strawberry topping:
    1. Top with sliced strawberries and optional warmed strawberry jam.
    2. Serve chilled and enjoy!

Enjoy your homemade Strawberry Cheesecake!

Main Ingredients:

  • 1½ cups (150g) graham cracker crumbs
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • ½ cup (115g) unsalted butter, melted
  • 24 oz (680g) cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup (200g) granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 large eggs
  • ½ cup (120ml) sour cream
  • 1 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1½ cups (300g) fresh strawberries, sliced
  • ½ cup (120ml) strawberry jam (optional for topping)
