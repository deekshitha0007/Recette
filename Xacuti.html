Xacuti Recipe - Goa

Procedure

  1. Dry roast spices (coriander, cumin, fennel, cloves, cinnamon) and grind with coconut.
  2. Marinate chicken with turmeric, chili powder, and salt.
  3. Saut√© onions, ginger, garlic in oil till golden.
  4. Add ground spice-coconut paste and cook till oil separates.
  5. Add chicken and cook till done and tender.
  6. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot.

Enjoy your traditional Goa Xacuti!

Nutrition Per Serving:

Nutrient Amount
Calories 350 kcal
Carbohydrates 8g
Proteins 28g

Fibers 3g

Main Ingredients:

  • Chicken - 500g
  • Dry roasted spices
  • Grated coconut - 1 cup
  • Onions - 2 medium
  • Ginger-garlic paste - 2 tbsp
  • Oil - 3 tbsp
  • Turmeric, chili powder, salt
