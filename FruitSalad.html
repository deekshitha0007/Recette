Fresh Fruit Salad Recipe

Procedure

  1. Prepare the Fruits
    1. Wash all fruits thoroughly under cold running water.
    2. Pat dry with clean paper towels.
  2. Cut and Dice
    1. Cut strawberries into quarters, remove stems.
    2. Dice apples and pears into bite-sized pieces.
    3. Cut grapes in half if large.
    4. Peel and segment oranges, remove seeds.
  3. Make the Dressing
    1. In a small bowl, mix honey, lemon juice, and lime juice.
    2. Add a pinch of salt and whisk until combined.
    3. Optionally add fresh mint leaves, finely chopped.
  4. Combine and Serve
    1. Place all cut fruits in a large serving bowl.
    2. Pour the honey-citrus dressing over the fruits.
    3. Gently toss to coat evenly.
    4. Chill for 30 minutes before serving for best flavor.

Enjoy your refreshing and healthy Fruit Salad!

Nutrition Per Serving:

Nutrient Amount
Calories 120 kcal
Carbohydrates 31g
Proteins 2g
Fats 0.5g
Fibers 4g

Main Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Strawberries (hulled and quartered)
  • 2 medium Apples (diced)
  • 1 cup Green Grapes
  • 2 medium Oranges (peeled and segmented)
  • 1 cup Pineapple Chunks
  • 1 medium Pear (diced)
  • 2 tbsp Honey
  • 2 tbsp Fresh Lemon Juice
  • 1 tbsp Fresh Lime Juice
  • Fresh Mint Leaves (optional)
