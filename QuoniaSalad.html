Healthy Quinoa Salad Recipe

Procedure

  1. Cook the Quinoa
    1. Rinse quinoa thoroughly under cold water until water runs clear.
    2. In a saucepan, bring 2 cups water to boil and add quinoa.
    3. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 15 minutes until water is absorbed.
    4. Let cool completely and fluff with a fork.
  2. Prepare the Vegetables
    1. Dice cucumber and bell peppers into small pieces.
    2. Halve cherry tomatoes.
    3. Finely chop red onion and fresh herbs.
  3. Make the Dressing
    1. In a small bowl, whisk together lemon juice and olive oil.
    2. Add minced garlic, salt, pepper, and cumin.
    3. Whisk until well emulsified.
  4. Assemble the Salad
    1. In a large bowl, combine cooled quinoa with all vegetables.
    2. Add fresh herbs and crumbled feta cheese.
    3. Pour dressing over and toss well to combine.
    4. Let marinate for 30 minutes before serving for best flavor.

Enjoy your nutritious and protein-packed Quinoa Salad!

Nutrition Per Serving:

Nutrient Amount
Calories 320 kcal
Carbohydrates 42g
Proteins 12g
Fats 12g
Fibers 6g

Main Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Quinoa (uncooked)
  • 1 medium Cucumber (diced)
  • 1 cup Cherry Tomatoes (halved)
  • 1 Red Bell Pepper (diced)
  • 1/4 cup Red Onion (finely chopped)
  • 1/2 cup Feta Cheese (crumbled)
  • 1/4 cup Fresh Parsley (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp Fresh Mint (chopped)
  • 1/4 cup Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 3 tbsp Fresh Lemon Juice
  • 2 cloves Garlic (minced)
  • 1/2 tsp Ground Cumin
  • Salt and Black Pepper to taste
