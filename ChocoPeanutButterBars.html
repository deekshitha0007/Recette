Choco-Peanut Butter Bars

Procedure

  1. Prepare the base
    1. In a large bowl, mix the melted butter and peanut butter until smooth.
    2. Add the powdered sugar and graham cracker crumbs, then stir until combined.
  2. Press the mixture
    1. Line an 8x8 inch pan with parchment paper or grease it lightly.
    2. Press the peanut butter mixture evenly into the bottom of the pan.
  3. Make the chocolate topping
    1. In a microwave-safe bowl, melt the chocolate chips with the vegetable oil in 30-second intervals, stirring in between until smooth.
    2. Pour the melted chocolate over the peanut butter base and spread evenly.
  4. Chill and serve
    1. Refrigerate the bars for at least 1 hour or until firm.
    2. Cut into squares and serve.

Enjoy your homemade Choco-Peanut Butter Bars!

Main Ingredients:

  • 1 cup peanut butter (creamy or chunky)
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 cups powdered sugar
  • 2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1 1/2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil (optional, for smoother chocolate)
