Idli with Coconut Chutney Recipe

Procedure

  1. Prepare Idli Batter
    1. Soak urad dal and rice separately for 4-6 hours.
    2. Grind urad dal to a smooth, fluffy batter. Grind rice to a slightly coarse batter.
    3. Mix both batters with salt and ferment for 8-12 hours.
  2. Steam the Idlis
    1. Grease idli molds with oil or ghee.
    2. Pour batter into molds, filling 3/4th of each cavity.
    3. Steam for 12-15 minutes until cooked through.
  3. Make Coconut Chutney
    1. Grind coconut, green chilies, ginger, and salt with little water.
    2. Heat oil, add mustard seeds, curry leaves, and dried red chili.
    3. Pour tempering over chutney and mix well.
  4. Serve
    1. Remove idlis from molds carefully.
    2. Serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar.

Enjoy your soft and fluffy Idlis!

Nutrient Amount
Calories 220 kcal

Fibers 2g

Main Ingredients:

  • For Idli:
  • 2 cups Idli Rice
  • 1/2 cup Urad Dal
  • Salt to taste
  • For Coconut Chutney:
  • 1 cup Fresh Coconut (grated)
  • 2-3 Green Chilies
  • 1 inch Ginger
  • 1 tbsp Oil
  • 1 tsp Mustard Seeds
  • 8-10 Curry Leaves
  • 1 Dried Red Chili
  • Salt to taste
