Gustaba Recipe

Procedure

  1. Marinate mutton with yogurt and spices.
  2. Cook in a pressure cooker until tender.
  3. Prepare a rich gravy and serve hot.

Enjoy your Gustaba!

Nutrition Per Serving:

Nutrient Amount
Calories 500 kcal
Carbohydrates 10g
Proteins 40g
Fats 35g
Fibers 2g

Main Ingredients:

  • 500g Mutton
  • 1 cup Yogurt
  • Spices: Cardamom, Cloves, Cinnamon
