Hyderabadi Chicken Biryani Recipe

Procedure

  1. Marinate the Chicken
    1. Mix yogurt, red chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, ginger-garlic paste, and salt in a bowl.
    2. Add chicken pieces and marinate for 2-4 hours (overnight preferred).
  2. Prepare the Rice
    1. Soak basmati rice for 30 minutes, then boil with whole spices until 70% cooked.
    2. Drain and set aside.
  3. Cook the Chicken
    1. Heat ghee in a heavy-bottomed pot and fry onions until golden brown.
    2. Add marinated chicken and cook until tender.
    3. Layer the partially cooked rice over chicken.
  4. Dum Cooking
    1. Sprinkle fried onions, mint, saffron soaked in milk, and dots of ghee.
    2. Cover with aluminum foil, then lid. Cook on high for 3 minutes, then low heat for 45 minutes.

Enjoy your authentic Hyderabadi Chicken Biryani!

Nutrition Per Serving:

Nutrient Amount

Carbohydrates 65g
Proteins 28g
Fats 18g

Main Ingredients:

  • 500g Chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 2 cups Basmati Rice
  • 1 cup Yogurt
  • 3 large Onions (sliced)
  • 4 tbsp Ghee
  • 1/2 cup Fresh Mint Leaves
  • 1/4 tsp Saffron
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • Spices: Bay leaves, Cinnamon, Cardamom, Cloves, Red Chili Powder, Turmeric, Garam Masala, Salt
