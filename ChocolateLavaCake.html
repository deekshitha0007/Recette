Chocolate Lava Cake

Procedure

  1. Combine butter and chopped chocolate in a double boiler and melt over low heat while stirring until smooth. (Or microwave in 30-second intervals until melted.)
  2. In a large mixing bowl, beat the eggs, egg yolks, sugar, and salt on medium speed until the mixture is thick and pale yellow.
  3. Add the melted chocolate mixture and flour into the egg mixture.
  4. Gently fold the mixture until fully combined.
  5. Pour the batter into greased ramekins.
  6. Bake in a preheated oven at 425°F (220°C) for 6–8 minutes, until the sides are set but the center is still jiggly.
  7. Let the cakes cool in the ramekins for 1 minute.
  8. Place a dessert plate over each ramekin and carefully flip it to invert the lava cake onto the plate.
  9. Let sit for 10 seconds, then remove the ramekin.
  10. Dust with powdered sugar and garnish with berries if desired. Serve immediately.

Enjoy your homemade Chocolate Lava Cake!

Main Ingredients:

  • Unsalted butter
  • Bittersweet or semi-sweet high-quality chocolate (like Ghirardelli)
  • 2 whole eggs
  • 2 egg yolks
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Flour (small amount for structure)
  • Powdered sugar (for dusting)
  • Berries (optional, for garnish)
