Watermelon Basil Fizz Recipe

Procedure

  1. Prepare Watermelon Juice
    1. Cut watermelon into chunks and remove seeds.
    2. Blend watermelon chunks until smooth.
    3. Strain the juice to remove pulp if desired.
  2. Make Basil Simple Syrup
    1. Boil water and sugar in a small saucepan.
    2. Add fresh basil leaves and simmer for 2-3 minutes.
    3. Let it cool and strain out the basil leaves.
  3. Prepare the Fizz
    1. In a glass, add ice cubes and fresh basil leaves.
    2. Pour watermelon juice and basil syrup.
    3. Add a splash of lime juice.
  4. Serve
    1. Top with sparkling water or soda.
    2. Stir gently and garnish with watermelon cubes and basil sprig.
    3. Serve immediately while fizzy.

Enjoy your refreshing Watermelon Basil Fizz!

Nutrition Per Serving:

Nutrient Amount
Calories 85 kcal
Carbohydrates 22g
Proteins 1g
Fats 0g
Fibers 1g

Main Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Fresh Watermelon (cubed)
  • 10-12 Fresh Basil Leaves
  • 2 tbsp Sugar (for syrup)
  • 1/4 cup Water (for syrup)
  • 1 tbsp Fresh Lime Juice
  • 1/2 cup Sparkling Water
  • Ice Cubes
  • Extra basil sprigs for garnish
  • Watermelon cubes for garnish
