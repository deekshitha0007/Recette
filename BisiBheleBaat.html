Bisi Bele Bhaat Recipe - Karnataka

Procedure

  1. Cook Rice and Dal
    1. Cook rice and toor dal together with turmeric and salt until soft.
    2. Mash slightly and keep aside.
  2. Prepare Vegetables
    1. Chop mixed vegetables into small pieces.
    2. Cook vegetables in salted water until tender.
    3. Drain and keep aside.
  3. Make the Main Dish
    1. Heat ghee in a large pot, add mustard seeds and curry leaves.
    2. Add cooked rice-dal mixture and vegetables.
    3. Add bisi bele bhaat powder, tamarind paste, and jaggery.
    4. Simmer for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Final Tempering and Serve
    1. Heat ghee, add cashews and fry until golden.
    2. Pour over the bisi bele bhaat.
    3. Serve hot with papad and pickle.

Enjoy your authentic Karnataka Bisi Bele Bhaat!

Main Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Basmati Rice
  • 1/2 cup Toor Dal
  • 2 tbsp Bisi Bele Bhaat Powder
  • 1 tbsp Tamarind Paste
  • 1 tsp Jaggery
  • 1 cup Mixed Vegetables (carrot, beans, peas, brinjal)
  • 3 tbsp Ghee
  • 1 tsp Mustard Seeds
  • 10-12 Curry Leaves
  • 2 tbsp Cashews
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • Pinch of Asafoetida
  • Salt to taste
