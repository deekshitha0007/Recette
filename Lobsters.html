Lobsters Recipe - Andaman and Nicobar Islands

Procedure

  1. Clean and marinate lobsters with salt, chili powder, and lemon juice.
  2. Grill or steam lobsters until fully cooked.
  3. Prepare a spicy coconut-based gravy or butter garlic sauce as preferred.
  4. Serve hot with rice or bread.

Enjoy your traditional Andaman and Nicobar Islands Lobsters!

Nutrition Per Serving:

Nutrient Amount
Calories 250 kcal
Carbohydrates 1g
Proteins 30g
Fats 7g

Main Ingredients:

  • Lobsters - 500g
  • Butter or coconut milk
  • Chili powder, salt, lemon juice
  • Garlic and spices
