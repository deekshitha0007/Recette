Garlic Butter Shrimp Pizza Recipe

Procedure

  1. Prepare Garlic Butter Shrimp
    1. Clean and devein shrimp, pat dry with paper towels.
    2. Melt butter and sauté minced garlic until fragrant.
    3. Add shrimp, cook for 2-3 minutes per side until pink.
    4. Season with salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes.
  2. Make Garlic White Sauce
    1. In same pan, add more butter and minced garlic.
    2. Whisk in flour and cook for 1 minute.
    3. Gradually add milk, whisk until smooth and thickened.
    4. Season with salt, pepper, and Italian herbs.
  3. Assemble the Pizza
    1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
    2. Brush pizza base with olive oil and garlic.
    3. Spread garlic white sauce evenly on base.
    4. Add mozzarella cheese, cooked shrimp, and cherry tomatoes.
  4. Bake and Serve
    1. Bake for 12-15 minutes until crust is golden.
    2. Garnish with fresh parsley and lemon zest.
    3. Drizzle with lemon juice and serve immediately.

Enjoy your luxurious Garlic Butter Shrimp Pizza!

Main Ingredients:

  • 1 Pizza Base (12 inch)
  • 400g Large Shrimp (peeled & deveined)
  • 4 tbsp Butter
  • 6 Garlic Cloves (minced)
  • 2 tbsp All-Purpose Flour
  • 1/2 cup Milk
  • 1 1/2 cups Mozzarella Cheese
  • 1 cup Cherry Tomatoes (halved)
  • Fresh Parsley & Lemon
  • Red Pepper Flakes
  • Italian Herbs, Salt & Black Pepper
  • Olive Oil for brushing
