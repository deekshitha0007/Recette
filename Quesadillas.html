Quesadillas

Procedure

  1. Preheat the pan
    1. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat.
  2. Assemble the quesadilla
    1. Place one tortilla on a flat surface.
    2. Sprinkle 1/2 cup of cheese evenly over one half of the tortilla.
    3. Add your desired fillings on top of the cheese.
    4. Fold the tortilla in half to cover the fillings.
  3. Cook the quesadilla
    1. Add a little oil or butter to the skillet.
    2. Place the folded tortilla in the skillet and cook for 2–3 minutes per side, or until golden brown and cheese is melted.
    3. Repeat with remaining tortillas and fillings.
  4. Slice and serve
    1. Cut each quesadilla into wedges using a knife or pizza cutter.
    2. Serve hot with sour cream, salsa, guacamole, and lime wedges.

Enjoy your homemade Quesadillas!

Nutrient Amount

Fibers 2g

Main Ingredients:

  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • 2 cups shredded cheese (cheddar, Monterey Jack, or Mexican blend)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil or butter
  • Optional fillings:
    • 1 cup cooked chicken, beef, or beans
    • 1/2 cup chopped onions or bell peppers (sautéed)
    • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
    • 1/4 cup sliced jalapeños
  • For serving (optional):
    • Sour cream
    • Guacamole
    • Salsa
    • Lime wedges
