Rogan Josh Recipe

Procedure

  1. Marinate the Meat
    1. Mix lamb pieces with yogurt, red chili powder, and salt.
    2. Marinate for at least 1 hour or overnight.
  2. Brown the Meat
    1. Heat ghee in a heavy-bottomed pot.
    2. Brown the marinated lamb pieces on all sides. Set aside.
  3. Prepare the Base
    1. In the same pot, add whole spices and fry until fragrant.
    2. Add onion paste and cook until golden brown.
    3. Add ginger-garlic paste and Kashmiri red chili paste.
  4. Slow Cook
    1. Return lamb to the pot, add water, and bring to boil.
    2. Simmer covered for 1.5-2 hours until tender. Garnish with cilantro.

Enjoy your homemade Rogan Josh!

Nutrition Per Serving:

Nutrient Amount

Main Ingredients:

  • 750g Lamb (cut into pieces)
  • 1/2 cup Yogurt
  • 3 tbsp Ghee
  • 2 Bay Leaves
  • 4-5 Green Cardamom
  • 2 inch Cinnamon Stick
  • 2 large Onions (paste)
  • 2 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 2 tbsp Kashmiri Red Chili Paste
  • Spices: Fennel Powder, Ginger Powder, Garam Masala, Salt
