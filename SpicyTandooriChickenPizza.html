Spicy Tandoori Chicken Pizza Recipe

Procedure

  1. Marinate Chicken
    1. Cut chicken into small bite-sized pieces.
    2. Mix yogurt, tandoori masala, ginger-garlic paste, and salt.
    3. Marinate chicken for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Cook Tandoori Chicken
    1. Heat oil in a pan and cook marinated chicken.
    2. Cook until chicken is fully done and slightly charred.
    3. Set aside to cool.
  3. Assemble the Pizza
    1. Preheat oven to 450°F (230°C).
    2. Spread pizza sauce mixed with some tandoori masala.
    3. Add mozzarella cheese and tandoori chicken pieces.
    4. Top with red onions, bell peppers, and jalapeños.
  4. Bake and Serve
    1. Bake for 15-18 minutes until crust is golden.
    2. Garnish with fresh cilantro and onion rings.
    3. Drizzle with mint chutney and serve hot.

Enjoy your spicy Tandoori Chicken Pizza!

Nutrition Per Slice (8 slices):

Nutrient Amount
Calories 380 kcal
Carbohydrates 35g
Proteins 22g
Fats 18g
Fibers 2g

Main Ingredients:

  • 1 Pizza Base (12 inch)
  • 300g Chicken Breast (cubed)
  • 1/2 cup Plain Yogurt
  • 2 tbsp Tandoori Masala
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 1 1/2 cups Mozzarella Cheese
  • 1/2 cup Pizza Sauce
  • 1 Red Onion (sliced)
  • 1/2 Bell Pepper (sliced)
  • 2-3 Jalapeños (sliced)
  • Fresh Cilantro & Mint Chutney
  • Salt & Cooking Oil
