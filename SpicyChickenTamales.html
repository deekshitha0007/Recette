Spicy Chicken Tamales

Procedure

  1. Prepare the filling
    1. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
    2. Saut√© onion and garlic until fragrant.
    3. Add shredded chicken, enchilada sauce, chili powder, and salt. Stir and simmer for 10 minutes. Set aside.
  2. Make the masa dough
    1. In a bowl, beat shortening until fluffy.
    2. In a separate bowl, mix masa harina, baking powder, and salt.
    3. Add dry ingredients to the shortening gradually, alternating with warm chicken broth. Mix until dough is soft and holds its shape when spread.
  3. Assemble the tamales
    1. Drain and pat dry the corn husks.
    2. Spread about 2 tablespoons of masa dough on the wide end of a corn husk.
    3. Place 1 tablespoon of chicken filling in the center.
    4. Fold sides of the husk over the filling, then fold the bottom up to seal.
  4. Steam the tamales
    1. Place tamales upright in a steamer basket with open ends facing up.
    2. Steam over simmering water for 1 to 1.5 hours, checking water level regularly.
    3. Tamales are done when the masa pulls away easily from the husk.
  5. Serve
    1. Let tamales rest for 10 minutes after steaming.
    2. Serve with salsa, sour cream, or guacamole.

Enjoy your homemade Spicy Chicken Tamales!

Main Ingredients:

  • For the filling:
    • 2 cups cooked shredded chicken
    • 1 tablespoon olive oil
    • 1 small onion, finely chopped
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 cup red enchilada sauce or spicy salsa
    • 1 teaspoon chili powder
    • Salt to taste
  • For the masa dough:
    • 2 cups masa harina
    • 1/2 cup vegetable shortening or lard
    • 1 1/2 cups chicken broth (warm)
    • 1 teaspoon baking powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 20 dried corn husks, soaked in warm water for 30 minutes
