Chole Bhature Recipe - Delhi

Procedure

  1. Soak chickpeas overnight and pressure cook until soft.
  2. Saut√© onions, tomatoes, ginger-garlic paste, and spices to make chole gravy.
  3. Add cooked chickpeas and simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  4. Prepare bhature dough with flour, yogurt, and yeast, let it rise.
  5. Roll and deep fry bhature until puffed and golden.
  6. Serve hot with chole and garnish with onions and lemon.

Enjoy your traditional Delhi Chole Bhature!

Main Ingredients:

  • Chickpeas - 1 cup
  • Onion, tomato
  • Wheat flour - 2 cups
  • Yeast, yogurt
  • Spices (chili, cumin, coriander)
  • Oil for frying
