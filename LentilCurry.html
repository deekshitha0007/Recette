Lentil Curry Recipe

Procedure

  1. Prepare the Lentils
    1. Wash and soak lentils for 20 minutes.
    2. Boil lentils with turmeric and salt until soft and mushy.
  2. Make the Tempering (Tadka)
    1. Heat ghee in a pan and add cumin seeds, mustard seeds.
    2. Add dried red chilies, curry leaves, and asafoetida.
  3. Build the Curry
    1. Add onions to the tempering and saut√© until golden.
    2. Add ginger-garlic paste, tomatoes, and cook until soft.
    3. Add spices and cook until fragrant.
  4. Combine and Finish
    1. Add cooked lentils to the spice mixture.
    2. Simmer for 10-15 minutes, garnish with cilantro.

Enjoy your homemade Lentil Curry!

Main Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Red Lentils (Masoor Dal)
  • 2 tbsp Ghee
  • 1 tsp Cumin Seeds
  • 1 tsp Mustard Seeds
  • 1 large Onion (chopped)
  • 2 Tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 2 Dried Red Chilies
  • 8-10 Curry Leaves
  • Spices: Turmeric, Red Chili Powder, Coriander Powder, Salt
