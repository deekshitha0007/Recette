Banana Bread Recipe

Procedure

  1. Preheat and prepare:
    1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
    2. Grease a 4x8 inch loaf pan or line with parchment paper.
  2. Mix wet ingredients:
    1. Mash the bananas in a large mixing bowl with a fork.
    2. Stir in the melted butter.
    3. Mix in sugar, beaten egg, and vanilla extract.
  3. Mix dry ingredients:
    1. Sprinkle baking soda and salt over the mixture.
    2. Add flour and stir until just combined (don’t overmix).
  4. Bake the bread:
    1. Pour batter into the prepared loaf pan.
    2. Bake for 50–60 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  5. Cool and serve:
    1. Let the bread cool in the pan for about 10 minutes.
    2. Remove from pan and allow to cool completely on a wire rack.
    3. Slice and enjoy!

Enjoy your homemade Banana Bread!

Main Ingredients:

  • 2 to 3 ripe bananas, mashed
  • ⅓ cup (75g) melted butter
  • ½ cup (100g) sugar (you can reduce to ⅓ cup for less sweetness)
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1½ cups (190g) all-purpose flour
