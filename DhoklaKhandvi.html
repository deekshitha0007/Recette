Dhokla Khandvi Recipe - Gujarat

Procedure

  1. Prepare Dhokla Batter
    1. Mix gram flour, yogurt, water, ginger-chili paste, and salt.
    2. Add fruit salt and steam for 15-20 minutes until spongy.
  2. Make Khandvi Batter
    1. Mix gram flour and yogurt with water to smooth consistency.
    2. Cook on low heat, stirring continuously until thick.
    3. Spread thin on greased surface and roll when cool.
  3. Prepare Tempering
    1. Heat oil in pan, add mustard seeds and curry leaves.
    2. Add green chilies and sesame seeds.
    3. Pour over both dhokla and khandvi.
  4. Final Garnish and Serve
    1. Cut dhokla into squares and arrange khandvi rolls.
    2. Garnish with fresh coriander and grated coconut.
    3. Serve with green chutney and sweet chutney.

Enjoy your authentic Gujarat Dhokla Khandvi!

Main Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Gram Flour (Besan)
  • 1 cup Yogurt
  • 1 tsp Ginger-Green Chili Paste
  • 1 tsp Fruit Salt (Eno)
  • 1 tsp Mustard Seeds
  • 1 tsp Sesame Seeds
  • 10-12 Curry Leaves
  • 2-3 Green Chilies
  • 2 tbsp Oil
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • Fresh Coriander Leaves
  • Grated Coconut for garnish
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as required
