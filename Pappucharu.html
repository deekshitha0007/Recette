Pappucharu Recipe - Andhra Pradesh

Procedure

  1. Prepare Tamarind Base
    1. Soak tamarind in warm water for 15 minutes.
    2. Extract thick tamarind juice and strain.
    3. Keep aside for later use.
  2. Cook Vegetables
    1. Heat oil in a heavy-bottomed pan.
    2. Add vegetables like drumstick, brinjal, okra.
    3. Cook until half done and set aside.
  3. Make the Curry
    1. In the same pan, add mustard seeds, cumin, and curry leaves.
    2. Add onions and cook until translucent.
    3. Add tamarind juice, rasam powder, turmeric, and salt.
    4. Add cooked vegetables and simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. Final Touch and Serve
    1. Adjust seasoning and consistency with water if needed.
    2. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
    3. Serve hot with steamed rice.

Enjoy your tangy Andhra Pradesh Pappucharu!

Main Ingredients:

  • 1 large lemon-sized Tamarind
  • 2 tbsp Rasam Powder
  • 1 cup Mixed Vegetables (drumstick, brinjal, okra)
  • 1 large Onion (chopped)
  • 3-4 Tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 tsp Mustard Seeds
  • 1 tsp Cumin Seeds
  • 10-12 Curry Leaves
  • 2-3 Dry Red Chilies
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 2 tbsp Coconut Oil
  • Fresh Coriander Leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as required
