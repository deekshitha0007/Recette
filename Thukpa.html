Thukpa Recipe - Sikkim

Procedure

  1. Boil broth with vegetables like carrots, beans, tomatoes, and greens.
  2. Add cooked noodles and simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Add soy sauce, salt, pepper, and chili for taste.
  4. Garnish with spring onions and serve hot.

Enjoy your traditional Sikkim Thukpa!

Nutrition Per Serving:

Nutrient Amount
Calories 200 kcal
Carbohydrates 40g
Proteins 60g
Fats 3g

Main Ingredients:

  • Noodles - 150g
  • Mixed vegetables
  • Vegetable broth
  • Soy sauce, salt, pepper, chili
  • Spring onions
