Mutton Biryani Recipe

Procedure

  1. Marinate the Mutton
    1. Mix yogurt, red chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, ginger-garlic paste, and salt.
    2. Marinate mutton pieces for 4-6 hours or overnight for best results.
  2. Prepare the Rice
    1. Soak basmati rice for 45 minutes.
    2. Boil with whole spices and salt until 70% cooked, then drain.
  3. Cook the Mutton
    1. Heat ghee and fry onions until deep golden brown.
    2. Add marinated mutton and cook on medium heat until tender (45-60 minutes).
    3. Add fried onions back to the mutton.
  4. Dum Cooking
    1. Layer the rice over cooked mutton in heavy-bottomed pot.
    2. Top with mint, coriander, saffron milk, and ghee.
    3. Seal with aluminum foil and lid. Cook on high for 4 minutes, then low heat for 50 minutes.

Enjoy your royal Mutton Biryani!

Nutrition Per Serving:

Nutrient Amount
Calories 580 kcal
Carbohydrates 62g
Proteins 35g
Fats 22g
Fibers 2g

Main Ingredients:

  • 600g Mutton (cut into pieces)
  • 2 cups Basmati Rice
  • 1 cup Yogurt
  • 4 large Onions (sliced)
  • 5 tbsp Ghee
  • 1/2 cup Fresh Mint Leaves
  • 1/4 cup Coriander Leaves
  • 1/4 tsp Saffron
  • 2 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • Spices: Bay leaves, Cinnamon, Green Cardamom, Black Cardamom, Cloves, Red Chili Powder, Turmeric, Garam Masala, Salt
