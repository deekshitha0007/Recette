Misal Pav Recipe - Maharashtra

Procedure

  1. Prepare the Misal Base
    1. Soak mixed sprouts overnight and boil them until tender.
    2. Heat oil in a pan and add mustard seeds, cumin seeds.
    3. Add onions, ginger-garlic paste, and green chilies. Saut√© until golden.
  2. Make the Spicy Gravy
    1. Add tomatoes and cook until soft.
    2. Add red chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, and salt.
    3. Add boiled sprouts and mix well.
  3. Prepare the Garnish
    1. Finely chop onions, tomatoes, and coriander leaves.
    2. Prepare farsan (mixture) and lemon wedges.
  4. Serve
    1. Serve hot misal in bowls.
    2. Top with chopped onions, tomatoes, farsan, and coriander.
    3. Serve with buttered pav and lemon wedges.

Enjoy your authentic Maharashtra Misal Pav!

Main Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Mixed Sprouts (moth beans, mung beans)
  • 2 large Onions (1 for cooking, 1 for garnish)
  • 2 Tomatoes (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 2-3 Green Chilies
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • 4-6 Pav Bread
  • Farsan (Bombay Mix) for garnish
  • Fresh Coriander Leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for cooking
