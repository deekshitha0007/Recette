Paneer Tikka Masala Recipe

Procedure

  1. Marinate the Paneer
    1. In a bowl, mix yogurt, lemon juice, turmeric, red chili powder, garam masala, ginger-garlic paste, and salt.
    2. Add paneer cubes and bell peppers. Marinate for 30 minutes.
  2. Grill the Paneer
    1. Thread paneer and vegetables on skewers.
    2. Grill or pan-fry until lightly charred. Set aside.
  3. Prepare the Masala
    1. Heat oil and saut√© onions until golden brown.
    2. Add ginger-garlic paste, tomato puree, and cook until oil separates.
    3. Add spices, cashew paste, cream, and simmer.
  4. Combine and Serve
    1. Add grilled paneer to the masala and simmer for 5 minutes.
    2. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot.

Enjoy your homemade Paneer Tikka Masala!

Nutrient Amount

Main Ingredients:

  • 400g Paneer (cubed)
  • 1/2 cup Yogurt
  • 2 tbsp Oil
  • 1 large Onion (chopped)
  • 3 Tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1/3 cup Heavy Cream
  • 1/4 cup Cashew Paste
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 1 Bell Pepper (cubed)
  • Spices: Turmeric, Red Chili Powder, Garam Masala, Cumin, Salt
