Kadalakka Recipe - Lakshadweep

Procedure

  1. Clean fish pieces and marinate with turmeric and salt.
  2. Prepare a masala paste using coconut, red chili, garlic, and spices.
  3. Saut√© onions and add masala paste, cook till oil separates.
  4. Add fish pieces and simmer in tamarind water until cooked.
  5. Garnish with curry leaves and serve hot with rice.
  6. Garnish with herbs or accompaniments and serve hot.

Enjoy your traditional Lakshadweep Kadalakka!

Main Ingredients:

  • Fresh fish - 500g
  • Grated coconut - 1 cup
  • Red chili, garlic, tamarind
  • Onion, curry leaves
  • Turmeric, salt
