Mishti Doi Recipe - West Bengal

Procedure

  1. Prepare Caramelized Sugar
    1. Heat sugar in a heavy-bottomed pan until it melts and turns golden.
    2. Add a little water carefully to make caramel syrup.
    3. Let it cool slightly and set aside.
  2. Heat and Reduce Milk
    1. Boil full-fat milk in a heavy-bottomed pan.
    2. Reduce heat and simmer until milk reduces to 3/4th quantity.
    3. Add caramel syrup and mix well.
  3. Add Culture
    1. Cool the milk to lukewarm temperature.
    2. Whisk in yogurt or buttermilk as starter culture.
    3. Mix thoroughly to avoid lumps.
  4. Set and Serve
    1. Pour into earthen pots or glass containers.
    2. Cover and keep in a warm place for 6-8 hours to set.
    3. Refrigerate and serve chilled with nuts if desired.

Enjoy your sweet Bengal Mishti Doi!

Main Ingredients:

  • 1 liter Full-fat Milk
  • 1/2 cup Sugar (for caramelization)
  • 2 tbsp Fresh Yogurt or Buttermilk
  • Optional: Chopped Pistachios
  • Optional: Almonds for garnish
  • Earthen pots (Matka) for authentic taste
