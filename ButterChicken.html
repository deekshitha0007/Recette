Butter Chicken Recipe

Procedure

  1. Marinate the Chicken
    1. In a bowl, mix yogurt, lemon juice, turmeric, red chili powder, garam masala, ginger-garlic paste, and salt.
    2. Add chicken pieces and coat well. Marinate for 1 hour (overnight for best flavor).
  2. Cook the Chicken
    1. Heat oil in a pan and cook marinated chicken until lightly charred.
    2. Set aside.
  3. Prepare the Gravy
    1. Melt butter and saut√© onions until golden brown.
    2. Add ginger-garlic paste, tomato puree, and cook until oil separates.
    3. Add spices, cashew paste, fresh cream, and honey.
  4. Combine and Serve
    1. Add cooked chicken to the gravy and simmer for 5-10 minutes.
    2. Garnish with coriander leaves and drizzle with cream.

Enjoy your homemade Butter Chicken!

Nutrition Per Serving:

Nutrient Amount

Main Ingredients:

  • 500g Boneless Chicken
  • 1/2 cup Yogurt
  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • 1 large Onion (chopped)
  • 2 Tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1/2 cup Fresh Cream
  • 1/4 cup Cashew Paste
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • Spices: Turmeric, Red Chili Powder, Garam Masala, Salt
