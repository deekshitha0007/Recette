Bafauri Recipe

Procedure

  1. Soak chana dal overnight and grind it to a coarse paste.
  2. Add spices, salt, and mix well.
  3. Shape the mixture into small balls and steam them.

Enjoy your Bafauri!

Nutrition Per Serving:

Nutrient Amount
Calories 200 kcal
Carbohydrates 30g
Proteins 10g
Fats 5g
Fibers 4g

Main Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Chana Dal
  • 1 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
