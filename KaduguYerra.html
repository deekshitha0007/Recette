Kadugu Yerra Recipe - Puducherry

Procedure

  1. Marinate prawns with turmeric and salt.
  2. Grind mustard seeds with red chilies and coconut to a paste.
  3. Saut√© onions and curry leaves, add the mustard paste and cook till oil separates.
  4. Add prawns and cook till done.
  5. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve with rice.

Enjoy your traditional Puducherry Kadugu Yerra!
