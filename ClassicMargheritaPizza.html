Classic Margherita Pizza Recipe

Procedure

  1. Prepare Pizza Dough
    1. Mix flour, yeast, salt, and sugar in a bowl.
    2. Add warm water and olive oil, knead until smooth.
    3. Let rise for 1-2 hours until doubled in size.
  2. Make Pizza Sauce
    1. Heat olive oil and sauté minced garlic.
    2. Add crushed tomatoes, salt, and Italian herbs.
    3. Simmer for 15-20 minutes until thickened.
  3. Assemble the Pizza
    1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
    2. Roll out dough and place on greased pizza pan.
    3. Spread sauce evenly, leaving border for crust.
    4. Add mozzarella slices and fresh basil leaves.
  4. Bake and Serve
    1. Bake for 12-15 minutes until crust is golden.
    2. Drizzle with olive oil and add fresh basil.
    3. Slice and serve hot.

Enjoy your authentic Margherita Pizza!

Nutrient Amount

Main Ingredients:

  • For Dough:
  • 2 cups All-Purpose Flour
  • 1 tsp Active Dry Yeast
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp Sugar
  • 3/4 cup Warm Water
  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  • For Toppings:
  • 1/2 cup Pizza Sauce
  • 8 oz Fresh Mozzarella (sliced)
  • Fresh Basil Leaves
  • 2 Garlic Cloves (for sauce)
  • 1 can Crushed Tomatoes
  • Italian Herbs & Extra Olive Oil
