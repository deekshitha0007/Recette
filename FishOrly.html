Fish Orly Recipe - Odisha

Procedure

  1. Prepare the Fish
    1. Clean fish fillets and cut into medium-sized pieces.
    2. Marinate with lemon juice, salt, and turmeric for 20 minutes.
  2. Make the Batter
    1. Mix all-purpose flour, cornflour, and rice flour.
    2. Add ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, and salt.
    3. Add water gradually to make smooth, thick batter.
  3. Coat and Fry
    1. Heat oil in deep pan for frying.
    2. Dip fish pieces in batter, ensuring even coating.
    3. Deep fry until golden brown and crispy.
  4. Serve
    1. Drain on paper towels to remove excess oil.
    2. Garnish with onion rings and lemon wedges.
    3. Serve hot with mint chutney or ketchup.

Enjoy your crispy Odisha Fish Orly!

Nutrition Per Serving:

Nutrient Amount
Calories 320 kcal

Proteins 28g
Fats 18g
Omega-3 1.2g

Main Ingredients:

  • 500g Fresh Fish Fillets (Rohu or Pomfret)
  • 1/2 cup All-purpose Flour
  • 2 tbsp Cornflour
  • 2 tbsp Rice Flour
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 2 tbsp Lemon Juice
  • Oil for deep frying
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as required
  • Onion rings for garnish
  • Lemon wedges for serving
