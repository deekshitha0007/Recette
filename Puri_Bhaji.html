Puri Bhaji Recipe - Daman and Diu

Procedure

  1. Prepare puri dough with wheat flour, salt, and water.
  2. Roll puris into small circles and deep fry till puffed and golden.
  3. Boil potatoes, mash them, and saut√© with mustard seeds, turmeric, green chili, and coriander to make bhaji.
  4. Serve hot puris with the bhaji.

Enjoy your traditional Daman and Diu Puri Bhaji!

Nutrition Per Serving:

Nutrient Amount

Main Ingredients:

  • Wheat flour - 2 cups
  • Potatoes - 3 medium
  • Mustard seeds, turmeric, green chili
  • Oil for frying and tempering
  • Coriander leaves
