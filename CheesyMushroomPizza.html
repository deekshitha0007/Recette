Cheesy Mushroom Pizza Recipe

Procedure

  1. Prepare Mushrooms
    1. Clean and slice button mushrooms thinly.
    2. Heat butter in a pan and sauté mushrooms until golden.
    3. Season with salt, pepper, and Italian herbs.
  2. Prepare Pizza Base
    1. Roll out pizza dough on floured surface.
    2. Transfer to greased pizza pan or baking sheet.
    3. Create raised edges for the crust.
  3. Assemble the Pizza
    1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
    2. Spread white sauce or pizza sauce on base.
    3. Layer with mozzarella and cheddar cheese.
    4. Top with sautéed mushrooms and caramelized onions.
  4. Bake and Serve
    1. Bake for 12-15 minutes until cheese is golden and bubbly.
    2. Sprinkle with fresh thyme and black pepper.
    3. Let cool for 2 minutes, slice and serve.

Enjoy your gooey Cheesy Mushroom Pizza!

Nutrition Per Slice (8 slices):

Nutrient Amount
Calories 295 kcal
Carbohydrates 32g
Proteins 14g
Fats 14g
Fibers 2g

Main Ingredients:

  • 1 Pizza Base (12 inch)
  • 300g Button Mushrooms (sliced)
  • 1 1/2 cups Mozzarella Cheese
  • 1/2 cup Cheddar Cheese
  • 1/2 cup White Sauce or Pizza Sauce
  • 1 large Onion (caramelized)
  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • 2 Garlic Cloves (minced)
  • Fresh Thyme Leaves
  • Italian Herbs, Salt & Black Pepper
  • Olive Oil for drizzling
