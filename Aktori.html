Aktori Recipe

Procedure

  1. Prepare a dough with wheat flour and water.
  2. Stuff with a mixture of vegetables and spices.
  3. Cook on a hot griddle until golden brown.

Enjoy your Aktori!

Nutrition Per Serving:

Nutrient Amount
Calories 350 kcal
Carbohydrates 50g
Proteins 10g
Fats 15g
Fibers 4g

Main Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Wheat Flour
  • 1 cup Mixed Vegetables
  • Spices: Salt, Turmeric, Red Chili Powder
