Loaded Meat Feast Pizza

Procedure

  1. Preheat your oven until the stone reads 750 °F (400 °C)
    1. You can check this quickly, accurately and from a safe distance with an infrared thermometer.
  2. In a bowl, mix a pinch of sea salt into the crushed tomatoes.
    1. Set aside.
  3. Place one dough ball on your lightly floured work surface and begin pushing the air from the center out to the edge with your fingers.
    1. Stretch the dough into a 12-inch (30-centimeter) base and lay it on your countertop or lightly-floured pizza peel.
  4. Top the dough with 3 ounces (about 85 grams) of the tomato mixture, followed by the mozzarella, pepperoni, ham, Italian sausage and ground beef.
    1. Finish by sprinkling on the chopped bacon.
  5. Launch the pizza into the oven and cook for 60 to 90 seconds, turning it every 20 seconds for an even bake.
  6. Remove the pizza from the oven.
    1. Slice, serve and enjoy.

Enjoy your homemade Loaded Meat Feast Pizza!

Main Ingredients:

  • ⅓ cup (87 grams) crushed tomatoes
  • Jacobsens Trapani Sea Salt (1.8lb)
  • Fine sea salt, to taste
  • Flour, for dusting
  • ⅕ cup (15 grams) pepperoni
  • One 250-gram dough ball
  • ¾ cup (100 grams) low-moisture shredded mozzarella
  • ⅓ cup (30 grams) thick-sliced ham or Canadian bacon, chopped into squares
  • ¼ cup (30 grams) Italian sausage, cooked and crumbled
  • ⅓ cup (25 grams) ground beef, cooked and crumbled
  • 1 strip (12 grams) bacon, cooked and roughly chopped
