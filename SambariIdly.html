Sambar Idli Recipe - Tamil Nadu

Procedure

  1. Prepare Idli Batter
    1. Soak urad dal and rice separately for 6-8 hours.
    2. Grind to smooth batter, mix, and ferment overnight.
    3. Steam in idli moulds for 12-15 minutes.
  2. Cook Sambar Dal
    1. Pressure cook toor dal with turmeric and water until soft.
    2. Mash the dal and keep aside.
  3. Prepare Sambar
    1. Heat oil, add mustard seeds, curry leaves, and hing.
    2. Add vegetables, cook until tender.
    3. Add cooked dal, sambar powder, tamarind paste, and salt.
    4. Simmer until desired consistency.
  4. Serve
    1. Serve hot idlis with sambar.
    2. Garnish with fresh coriander.
    3. Serve with coconut chutney and ghee.

Enjoy your authentic Tamil Nadu Sambar Idli!

Main Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Idli Rice
  • 1/2 cup Urad Dal
  • 1 cup Toor Dal
  • 2 tbsp Sambar Powder
  • 1 tbsp Tamarind Paste
  • 1 Onion (chopped)
  • 2 Tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 cup Mixed Vegetables (drumstick, okra, brinjal)
  • 1 tsp Mustard Seeds
  • 10-12 Curry Leaves
  • Pinch of Asafoetida (Hing)
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • Fresh Coriander Leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for tempering
