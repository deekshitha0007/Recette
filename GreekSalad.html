Greek Salad Recipe

Procedure

  1. Prepare the Vegetables
    1. Cut tomatoes into wedges or large chunks.
    2. Slice cucumber into thick rounds or half-moons.
    3. Slice red onion into thin rings.
    4. Cut bell pepper into strips.
  2. Prepare the Feta and Olives
    1. Cut feta cheese into large cubes.
    2. Rinse olives if too salty.
    3. Set aside on separate plates.
  3. Make the Dressing
    1. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil and red wine vinegar.
    2. Add minced garlic, dried oregano, salt, and pepper.
    3. Whisk until well combined.
  4. Assemble and Serve
    1. Combine tomatoes, cucumber, onion, and bell pepper in a large bowl.
    2. Add olives and toss gently.
    3. Pour dressing over vegetables and mix well.
    4. Top with feta cheese cubes and fresh oregano.

Enjoy your authentic Mediterranean Greek Salad!

Nutrition Per Serving:

Nutrient Amount
Calories 280 kcal
Carbohydrates 12g
Proteins 8g
Fats 24g
Fibers 4g

Main Ingredients:

  • 4 large Tomatoes (cut into wedges)
  • 1 large Cucumber (sliced)
  • 1 medium Red Onion (thinly sliced)
  • 1 Green Bell Pepper (cut into strips)
  • 200g Feta Cheese (cubed)
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata Olives
  • 1/4 cup Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 2 tbsp Red Wine Vinegar
  • 2 cloves Garlic (minced)
  • 1 tsp Dried Oregano
  • Salt and Black Pepper to taste
